Rural Technology Action Group

Rural Technology Action Group (RuTAG) at Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IITD) was established in January 2009. It is located in eight IITs (Bombay, Delhi, Guwahati, Kanpur, Kharagpur, Madras, Roorkee, Ropar). The RuTAG cells are coordinated by the Office of the Principal Scientific Adviser (PSA) to the Govt. of India, New Delhi.
